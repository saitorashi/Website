Ragnarok is done. Endless winter has gripped the world. It's every man, dwarf, and demigod for themselves.

But a Second Sun is on the horizon, here to bring the world from the brink of a new ice age to a Regeneration... and then something terrible.

Trudy, a fugitive Valkyrie, carries the only hope the world has-the shield Svalinn. It's a race against time, the demons of Surt's burning legion, and some very persistent Valkyries. Will the world see a new green day, or will it all burn?
